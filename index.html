Traditional Food in Meghalaya

Shillong, Meghalaya

Embark on a culinary journey in Shillong, Meghalaya, where traditional food tantalizes the taste buds with a unique blend of flavors. From the iconic Jadoh, a fragrant rice and meat dish, to the hearty Smoked Pork with Bamboo Shoot, Meghalaya's traditional cuisine is a celebration of local ingredients and age-old cooking techniques. Indulge in the spicy allure of Dohkhlieh, a vibrant salad, or savor the delicacy of Bamboo Steamed Fish, showcasing the rich culinary heritage that defines Shillong's dining experience.

Double Decker Bridge

Cherrapunji, Meghalaya

The Double Decker Living Root Bridge in Meghalaya is a marvel of nature and human ingenuity, where ancient traditions intertwine with the lush landscapes. Crafted by the Khasi tribes using living roots of ancient rubber trees, this natural bridge stands as a testament to sustainable engineering. Spanning the Umshiang River in the village of Nongriat, the Double Decker Bridge enchants visitors with its ethereal beauty, offering a unique blend of architectural wonder and the serene ambiance of Meghalaya's dense rainforests.
